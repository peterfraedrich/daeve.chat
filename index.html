DAEVE is a group for the dads of Herndon MAEVE.

We wanted to provide a place for the dads to come together and have a community.

DAEVE is a Discord server designed to foster that community, help us get to know each other more, and maybe find some new friends along the way.

The server is free to join, we only ask that you be associated with a MAEVE member (for now). We may open this up in the future.

Join the server!
